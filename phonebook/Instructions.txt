How to start:
1) Open XAMP contoller and start Apache and MySQL.
2) Copy archive content into XAMP install direction /htdocs/
3) Open in browser phpMyAdmin panel, and add a new database "phonebook".
3) Again in phpMyAdmin panel, import the phonebook.sql file, located in the phonebook folder.
4) Site is ready to browse at - http://localhost/phoneBook/index.php
